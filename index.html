Eric Bonnet

Welcome to my home page. I'm a computational biologist working currently at the french Centre National de Recherche en Génomique Humaine (CNRGH, National Center for Human Genomic Research), located in Evry (France). The CNRGH is part of the french Commissariat à l'Énergie Atomique (CEA, Centre for Atomic Energy), and is one of the largest sequencing centres in Europe. It is dedicated to genomic research on human diseases. I am leading the computational genomics (bio-analysis) laboratory. We focus on the computational analysis of high-throughput genomic data related to human health and disease.
